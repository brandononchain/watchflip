# Authentication Checklist

## Before Purchase

- Verify reference number
- Verify serial range
- Check dial correctness
- Confirm bracelet and clasp codes
- Verify movement photos
- Check service history
- Avoid aftermarket parts
- Request timestamp photo
- Verify seller reputation

## Red Flags

- Price too low
- Seller refuses video call
- Stock-only photos
- Missing serial explanation
- Crypto-only payment pressure
